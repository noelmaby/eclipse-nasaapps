About Me

During a solar eclipse, the moon hides the sun's brilliance briefly, casting a shadow on Earth. In contrast, during a lunar eclipse, Earth passes between the sun and the moon, causing the moon to take on a reddish hue. Eclipses, celestial dances, occur when the Earth, moon, and sun align in perfect harmony. They are awe-inspiring reminders of the wonders of our solar system.
