Projects / Works

"Unity in Diversity" Campaign

Spearheaded a social initiative to promote unity among diverse communities. The campaign involved organizing cultural events, fostering dialogues, and celebrating shared traditions, leading to increased social cohesion and understanding.

"Onam Reimagined" Virtual Festival

In response to global challenges, I initiated the transition of the traditional Onam festival to a virtual platform. This project involved designing and coordinating virtual events, ensuring that the spirit of Onam continued to thrive despite physical limitations.

"Digital Empathy" UI/UX Design

Leveraged my UI/UX design skills to create user-centric digital interfaces. Focused on enhancing user experiences through intuitive design, resulting in improved user engagement and satisfaction.

"Legacy of Kerala" Cultural Preservation

Collaborated with cultural institutions to document and preserve Kerala's rich heritage. This project involved digitizing historical artifacts, archiving oral traditions, and curating exhibitions to ensure the legacy of Kerala endures.

"Community Empowerment" Education Program

Launched an educational program aimed at empowering underprivileged communities. Developed and implemented a curriculum, provided access to educational resources, and witnessed notable improvements in literacy rates and skills.

"Compassion in Action" Social Welfare Initiatives

Led various social welfare projects, including organizing food drives, healthcare camps, and disaster relief efforts. These initiatives underscore my commitment to the welfare of my people and the wider community.
